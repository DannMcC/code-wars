**7 kyu**

[Original Kata] (https://www.codewars.com/kata/525e5a1cb735154b320002c8)

**Description:**

Triangular numbers are so called because of the equilateral triangular shape that they occupy when laid out as dots. i.e.

`1st (1)   2nd (3)    3rd (6)
*          **        ***
           *         **
                     *`
